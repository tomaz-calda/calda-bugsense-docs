## What is BugSense?

BugSense is an internal bug-reporting platform built for agencies and freelancers. It gives your clients a simple way to submit bugs while keeping your team's workflow in Linear organized and transparent.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="For team leads" icon="users">
    Create projects linked to Linear, invite clients, and manage your bug pipeline with automated syncing and AI-generated reports.
  </Card>
  <Card title="For clients" icon="bug">
    Submit bug reports with screenshots and metadata, track issue status, leave comments, and receive weekly progress reports.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Linear">
    Team lead connects their Linear workspace via OAuth. BugSense syncs projects, teams, and issues bidirectionally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Link a Linear project to BugSense, assign a team lead, invite clients via email and link the project to a Slack channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clients report bugs">
    Clients submit issues through the app or SDK. Each issue is automatically created in Linear and checked for duplication using the OpenAI API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay in sync & get notified">
    Comments, status changes, and attachments sync between BugSense and Linear in real time via webhooks. Internal Slack channel receives notifications when new issues are created.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Weekly reports">
    Every Monday, BugSense generates AI-powered weekly reports based on progress in the previous week. Admins must review and approve the reports before they are visible to clients. They receive notifications via Slack when new reports are available.
  </Step>
</Steps>
