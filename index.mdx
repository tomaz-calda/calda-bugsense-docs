## What is BugSense?

BugSense is an internal bug-reporting platform built for agencies and freelancers. It gives your clients a simple way to submit bugs while keeping your team's workflow in Linear organized and transparent.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="For team leads" icon="users">
    Create projects linked to Linear, invite clients, and manage your bug pipeline with automated syncing and AI-generated reports.
  </Card>
  <Card title="For clients" icon="bug">
    Submit bug reports with screenshots and metadata, track issue status, leave comments, and receive weekly progress reports.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Linear">
    An admin connects their Linear workspace via OAuth. BugSense syncs projects, teams, and issues bidirectionally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Link a Linear project to BugSense, assign a team lead, and invite clients via email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clients report bugs">
    Clients submit issues through the app or SDK. Each issue is automatically created in Linear with a "Client view" label.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay in sync">
    Comments, status changes, and attachments sync between BugSense and Linear in real time via webhooks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Weekly reports">
    Every Monday, BugSense generates AI-powered weekly reports summarizing metrics, highlights, risks, and next steps for each project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
